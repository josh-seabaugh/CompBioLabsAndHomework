This is a recipe for chocolate chip cookies from an Epicurious.com article by Rhoda Boone (Full citation at end of recipe).

Ingredients:
	1.5 cups of flour
	1 tsp of baking powder
	0.5 tsp of salt
	0.25 tsp of baking soda
	2 eggs
	1 tsp of vanilla extract
	10 tbsp of room temperature butter
	0.75 cups of granulated sugar
	0.75 cups of brown sugar
	8 oz of chocolate chips

Instructions:
	Preheat oven to 375 F
	Whisk flour, baking powder, salt and baking soda in a bowl. Mix eggs and vanilla in a separate bowl.
	Using a mixer, beat butter, sugar and brown sugar until fluffy (3-4 min). 
	Add egg mix and beat until fluffy (3-4 min).
	Reduce mixer speed while slowly adding dry ingredients. Mix until incorporated.
	Fold in chocolate.
	Place ~12 tbsp of dough onto a baking sheet with parchment paper.
	Chill the dough on the baking sheet for about 15 minutes and then place in oven.
	Bake for around 10-12 minutes.
	Remove cookies from oven and allow to cool for ~3 minutes.


Boone, Rhoda. “Our Favorite Chocolate Chip Cookies.” Epicurious, Epicurious, 12 Feb. 2016, www.epicurious.com/recipes/food/views/our-favorite-chocolate-chip-cookies.
